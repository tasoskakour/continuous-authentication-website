First Use Case Scenario: A simulation of an online payment.

Hundreds of billions of dollars are transacted every year and the number is still rising. The online payment procedure is for good, a part of our lives and is making our financial businesses easier.
However, there are reported a lot of scams weekly from transactions and payments made by malicious people who didn't have authorized access to perform those transactions.
Fortunately, with the kind of continuous authentication system we offer, we hope that the number of scams will be reduced drastically, as the system will detect the user and classify him as an intruder as his keystroke patterns will deviate from the original user.

In this use case scenario, we will simulate a real online payment. Specifically, we will provide you to enter some of your personal info (your sensitive info can be fake), just like those which you are required to provide when you perform an online payment to purchase some product.
For this reason, we kindly ask you to fill the form below, by typing your personal information normally as you would type it on a real scenario.
